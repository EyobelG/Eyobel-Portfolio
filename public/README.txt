EYOBEL PORTFOLIO STATIC ASSETS
=============================

To make your real photo permanent:
1. Click the file explorer on the left of your AI Studio screen.
2. Upload your professional headshot image into this "public" folder.
3. Rename the uploaded file to "eyobel.jpg" (or "eyobel.png").

The portfolio application will automatically detect your uploaded photo and render it permanently, styled beautifully with 3D parallax effects, live weather, and time HUD widgets!
